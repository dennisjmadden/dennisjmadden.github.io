About Me

I've been studying human physiology in one form or another for ten years. First nutrition, then exercise, and finally environmental physiology. I'm a registered dietitian with a PhD in evidence-based medicine. Check out my CV and list of academic publications.

At various points in my life I've called myself a runner, triathlete, climber, cyclist, diver (SCUBA and springboard), skier and snowboarder. I like to exercise and get outside, I am always chasing some random fitness goal. I think running in the woods is my favorite.

I created anotherdamnfitnessblog.com to practice writing. The site is no longer up, but you can find the articles here.

Perhaps to balance out my physical identity, I secretly wish I was some sort of artist.

I consider myself a stoic and I believe in integrity, humility, and the beginner mindset.
